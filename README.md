<h1 align="center">AMQ Scripts</h1>
These are scripts for Anime Music Quiz game.

## [AMQ Shortcut+](https://github.com/easterechidna/amq-scripts/raw/main/amqShortcutPlus.user.js)
<div>
  <img src="Images/amqshortcutplus.png" alt="Image of the script" width="300">
  <br>
</div>
Allows you to type shortcuts for anime titles in the answer box.<br>
Supports multiple profiles, groups, and import/export.<br>
Autocomplete in the adding shortcut box will be available after entering the game.<br><br>
To access the settings, hover your mouse over the gear icon at the bottom right of the screen and click on “Shortcut+”.
